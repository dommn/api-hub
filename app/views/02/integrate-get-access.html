Get access

Most APIs are protected and require you to authenticate before use. Follow the provider’s onboarding process. This might include:

  • Requesting an API key or OAuth token
  • Signing a data-sharing agreement
  • Registering your system or service
  • Providing contact details and support arrangements

Use sandbox or test credentials where available, and confirm how you’ll access production when ready.
