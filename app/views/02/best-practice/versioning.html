Versioning

Why it's important

Versioning is crucial to maintain backwards compatibility when updating an API. It ensures that changes, such as new features or fixes, donâ€™t break existing users or clients who are dependent on previous versions. Versioning provides a clear and manageable way to evolve the API without causing disruption.

What it means

  • Use a clear versioning scheme, such as /api/v1/ and /api/v2/ in the URL path.
  • Inform users well in advance before deprecating or removing old versions.
  • Maintain backward compatibility where possible to avoid forcing users to adopt new versions unnecessarily.
