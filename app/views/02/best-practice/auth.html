Authentication and authorisation

Why it's important

Authentication and authorisation are fundamental to securing your API. They ensure that only authorized users can access the API and perform specific actions, protecting sensitive data and preventing unauthorised access.

What it means

  • Use OAuth 2.0 for secure authorisation and user identity verification.
  • Implement JWT (JSON Web Tokens) for token-based authentication.
