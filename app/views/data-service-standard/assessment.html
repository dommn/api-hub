Assessment Framework – Data Lens

A data-oriented GDS-style assessment might look like:

Discovery Assessment

  • Have you identified clear user needs for data?
  • Is the problem statement well-defined?
  • Are there ethical/privacy concerns with the data you're planning to collect/use?
  • Have you defined success criteria?

Alpha Assessment

  • Have you prototyped data flows or pipelines?
  • Have you tested with real users or data?
  • Are your data governance principles clear and enforced?
  • Do you have a plan for metadata, documentation, and versioning?

Beta Assessment

  • Can users find, understand, and use your data?
  • Have you tested the data service with a wide range of users?
  • Are data privacy and security handled appropriately?
  • Is the pipeline reliable and maintainable?

Live Assessment

  • Are you actively maintaining data quality and freshness?
  • Is user feedback being used to improve data outputs?
  • Are there processes for incident handling, lineage tracking, and auditing?
  • Are metrics being collected and reported?
